A comprehensive epidemiological model that extends measles transmission modeling with risk-\/stratified quarantine strategies.\hypertarget{md_docs__model_measles_mixing_risk_quarantine_autotoc_md19}{}\doxysection{Overview}\label{md_docs__model_measles_mixing_risk_quarantine_autotoc_md19}
{\ttfamily \mbox{\hyperlink{class_model_measles_mixing_risk_quarantine}{Model\+Measles\+Mixing\+Risk\+Quarantine}}} implements a measles transmission model based on the {\ttfamily \mbox{\hyperlink{class_model_measles_mixing}{Model\+Measles\+Mixing}}} framework, with enhanced quarantine policies that vary based on exposure risk levels. This allows for targeted public health interventions that can optimize resource allocation and epidemic control.\hypertarget{md_docs__model_measles_mixing_risk_quarantine_autotoc_md20}{}\doxysubsection{Key Features}\label{md_docs__model_measles_mixing_risk_quarantine_autotoc_md20}

\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item {\bfseries{Measles-\/specific disease progression}}\+: Susceptible → Exposed → Prodromal → Rash → Recovered
\item {\bfseries{Population mixing}}\+: Uses contact matrices to model heterogeneous mixing between population groups ~\newline

\item {\bfseries{Risk-\/stratified quarantine}}\+: Three-\/tier system (high/medium/low risk) with customizable durations
\item {\bfseries{Enhanced detection}}\+: Detection rate parameter that activates during quarantine periods
\item {\bfseries{Contact tracing}}\+: Comprehensive contact tracing with configurable success rates
\item {\bfseries{Entity-\/based risk assessment}}\+: Risk levels determined by shared entity membership
\item {\bfseries{Vaccination effects}}\+: Vaccine distribution with configurable efficacy
\end{DoxyItemize}\hypertarget{md_docs__model_measles_mixing_risk_quarantine_autotoc_md21}{}\doxysection{Disease States}\label{md_docs__model_measles_mixing_risk_quarantine_autotoc_md21}
The model includes 13 distinct states\+:


\begin{DoxyEnumerate}
\item {\bfseries{Susceptible}} -\/ Can become infected
\item {\bfseries{Exposed}} -\/ Infected but not yet infectious (incubation period) ~\newline

\item {\bfseries{Prodromal}} -\/ Infectious individuals (replaces \char`\"{}\+Infected\char`\"{} in standard SEIR)
\item {\bfseries{Rash}} -\/ Non-\/infectious with visible symptoms (detection occurs here)
\item {\bfseries{Isolated}} -\/ Detected individuals in self-\/isolation
\item {\bfseries{Isolated Recovered}} -\/ Recovered individuals still in isolation
\item {\bfseries{Detected Hospitalized}} -\/ Hospitalized individuals who were contact-\/traced
\item {\bfseries{Quarantined Exposed}} -\/ Exposed individuals in quarantine
\item {\bfseries{Quarantined Susceptible}} -\/ Susceptible individuals in quarantine
\item {\bfseries{Quarantined Prodromal}} -\/ Prodromal individuals in quarantine ~\newline

\item {\bfseries{Quarantined Recovered}} -\/ Recovered individuals in quarantine
\item {\bfseries{Hospitalized}} -\/ Individuals requiring hospital care
\item {\bfseries{Recovered}} -\/ Individuals who have recovered and gained immunity
\end{DoxyEnumerate}\hypertarget{md_docs__model_measles_mixing_risk_quarantine_autotoc_md22}{}\doxysection{Risk Classification System}\label{md_docs__model_measles_mixing_risk_quarantine_autotoc_md22}
When the quarantine process is triggered by a detected case, contacts are classified into three risk levels\+:\hypertarget{md_docs__model_measles_mixing_risk_quarantine_autotoc_md23}{}\doxysubsection{High Risk}\label{md_docs__model_measles_mixing_risk_quarantine_autotoc_md23}

\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item {\bfseries{Definition}}\+: Unvaccinated agents who share entity membership with the case that triggered quarantine
\item {\bfseries{Rationale}}\+: Highest transmission risk due to close, prolonged contact within same household/workplace/school
\item {\bfseries{Default quarantine duration}}\+: 21 days (configurable)
\end{DoxyItemize}\hypertarget{md_docs__model_measles_mixing_risk_quarantine_autotoc_md24}{}\doxysubsection{Medium Risk}\label{md_docs__model_measles_mixing_risk_quarantine_autotoc_md24}

\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item {\bfseries{Definition}}\+: Unvaccinated agents who had contact with infected individuals but don\textquotesingle{}t share entity membership
\item {\bfseries{Rationale}}\+: Moderate transmission risk from community contact
\item {\bfseries{Default quarantine duration}}\+: 14 days (configurable)
\end{DoxyItemize}\hypertarget{md_docs__model_measles_mixing_risk_quarantine_autotoc_md25}{}\doxysubsection{Low Risk}\label{md_docs__model_measles_mixing_risk_quarantine_autotoc_md25}

\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item {\bfseries{Definition}}\+: All other unvaccinated agents
\item {\bfseries{Rationale}}\+: Lowest transmission risk, potential for community spread
\item {\bfseries{Default quarantine duration}}\+: 7 days (configurable)
\end{DoxyItemize}

{\bfseries{Note}}\+: Vaccinated agents (those with tools) are not subject to quarantine regardless of contact patterns.\hypertarget{md_docs__model_measles_mixing_risk_quarantine_autotoc_md26}{}\doxysection{Enhanced Detection}\label{md_docs__model_measles_mixing_risk_quarantine_autotoc_md26}
