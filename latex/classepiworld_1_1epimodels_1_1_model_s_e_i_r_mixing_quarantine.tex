\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\subsubsection*{template$<$typename TSeq = EPI\+\_\+\+DEFAULT\+\_\+\+TSEQ$>$\newline
class epiworld\+::epimodels\+::\+Model\+SEIRMixing\+Quarantine$<$ TSeq $>$}

SEIR model with mixing, quarantine, and contact tracing. 

This class implements a Susceptible-\/\+Exposed-\/\+Infected-\/\+Removed (SEIR) epidemiological model with additional features including\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Population mixing based on contact matrices
\item Quarantine measures for exposed contacts
\item Isolation policies for detected infected individuals
\item Contact tracing with configurable success rates
\item Hospitalization of severe cases
\item Individual willingness to comply with public health measures
\end{DoxyItemize}

The model supports 10 distinct states\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Susceptible\+: Individuals who can become infected
\item Exposed\+: Infected but not yet infectious (incubation period)
\item Infected\+: Infectious individuals in the community
\item Isolated\+: Detected infected individuals in self-\/isolation
\item Detected Hospitalized\+: Hospitalized individuals who were contact-\/traced
\item Quarantined Susceptible\+: Susceptible individuals in quarantine due to contact tracing
\item Quarantined Exposed\+: Exposed individuals in quarantine due to contact tracing
\item Isolated Recovered\+: Recovered individuals still in isolation
\item Hospitalized\+: Individuals requiring hospital care
\item Recovered\+: Individuals who have recovered and gained immunity
\end{DoxyItemize}
